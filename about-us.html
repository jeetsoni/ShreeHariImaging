Shree Hari Imaging Center

Shree Hari Imaging centre is first full fledged imaging centre in Bavla. Our expert team will provide international standard state of art diagnostic services. Dr Nirav Adesara (Chief Radiologist ) has wide experienced in field of radiology. He has obtained his post graduation training in Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani Hospital in Mumbai which is one of the premier radiology institute. He has also worked in Sola civil Hospital and Sterling Hospital.
